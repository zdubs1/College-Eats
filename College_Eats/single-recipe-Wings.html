Air-Fry Wings

Get ready to elevate your wing game with our Easy and Delicious Air Fryer Wings recipe! Whether you're whipping up a quick weeknight dinner, hosting a game-day gathering, or simply craving a tasty treat, these wings are the ultimate go-to.

What makes these wings truly spectacular is the incredible ease of preparation without compromising on taste. With your trusty air fryer, you can achieve that perfect balance of crispy, golden-brown exterior and tender, juicy meat in a fraction of the time it takes to oven-bake or deep fry.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : American

instructions

step 1

Remove wings from the package, wash them off and pat them dry.

step 2

In a bowl add seasonings to the chicken and mix until seasoned evenly

step 3

Put the wings in the air fryer for 20-25 minutes or until crispy

step 4

Once the wings are finsihed let them rest for 3-5 minutes then add them to a mixing bowl.

step 5

Pour sauce into the mixing bowl and toss the wings, covering them in whatever sauce you please.

step 6

Plate and serve with ranch or blue cheese.
