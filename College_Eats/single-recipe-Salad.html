Fiesta Salad

"The Healthy Fiesta Salad" truly comes alive with the harmonious combination of sweet cherry tomatoes, creamy avocado, and succulent sweet corn. These colorful additions provide essential vitamins and fiber, making each forkful a step toward a healthier you.

To elevate the experience, we introduce zesty red onions and aromatic cilantro, adding zest to your salad without compromising its health benefits. A drizzle of tangy lime dressing complements the ingredients perfectly, delivering that classic Mexican-inspired taste with a nutritious twist.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Mexican Healthy

instructions

step 1

Chop lettuce for desired sald leaf size and add to bowl.

step 2

Dice Red onion, tomato, and avocado, and to the bowl of salad.

step 3

Open a can of corn and wash it off, once washed add to the salad bowl.

step 4

In a medium pan put 2 seasoned chicken breasts and cover flipping after 4 min. Once cooked slice evenly and add to the bowl.

step 5

Add Feta and ranch on top, serve in bowl or plate.

ingredients

Ingredients Ammounts
Iceberg lettuce: 2 Heads
Red Onion: 1 Whole
Avocado: 2 Whole
Tomato: 1 Whole
Corn: 1 Can
Chicken Breasts: 2 Whole
Salt: 1 tbsp
Pepper: 1 tbsp
Feta: 1 Container Crumbled
Ranch Dressing: 1/2 Cup

tools

Medium Fry Pan

Chopping Utensil

Cutting Board

Can opener

Measuring Cups
