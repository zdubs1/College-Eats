Spicy Ramen

Whether you're seeking comfort on a chilly evening or a bold and satisfying meal to awaken your taste buds, our Spicy Ramen Delight with Fried Chicken is the ultimate fusion of flavors that promises to transport you to a realm of culinary bliss. Prepare to embark on a gastronomic journey that tantalizes every sense, leaving you craving more of this unforgettable culinary creation

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Asain

instructions

step 1

In a bowl add cayenne pepper, salt, parika, onion powder, garlic powder, and flour. Mix until fully incorporated.

step 2

Go back and forth twice between egg wash and flower mixture, fully coating the chicken.

step 3

In a hot pan add oil, once oil is hot add the chicken and fry on each side until golden brown. Let Cool then cut into thin peices

step 4

While the chicken is cooking boil water and add two packages of ramen, add the ramen packet in while boiling.

step 5

In another pan add butter, gochujang, soy sauce, siracha, and seasame oil. let simmer until fully incorporated

step 6

Add the ramen noodles along with water to the sauce pan mix and incorporate. Then add chives and the sliced fried chicken on top.

ingredients

Ingredients Ammounts
Ramen: 2 packs
Thinly slice Chicken: 2 breasts
Flour: 1 cup
Egg: 3 Eggs
Salt: 1 tbsp
Garlic Powder: 1/2 tbsp
Pepper: 1 tbsp
Cayenne Pepper: to taste
Onion Powder: 1 tbsp
Butter: 1/2 stick
Siracha: 2 tbsp
Parika: 1 tbsp
Gochujang: 3 tbsp
Soy sauce: To taste
Seasame Oil: 2 tbsp
Chives: 1 Whole

tools

Large Sauce Pan

Cooking Utensil

Cutting Board

Knife for Cutting
