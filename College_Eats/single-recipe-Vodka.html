Spicy Rigatoni

Our Spicy Rigatoni recipe is not only a delight for the palate but also a feast for the eyes, with its vibrant red hues and enticing aroma that fills the kitchen. Whether you're craving a comforting meal with a fiery twist or impressing your guests with a bold Italian creation, this Spicy Rigatoni is sure to please even the most discerning of taste buds.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Italian

instructions

step 1

Dice onions finely and chop garlic for saute.

step 2

Melt Butter in a pan and add diced onions, garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook until translucent.

step 3

Add tomato paste to the pan with onions and cook down until fully incorporated.

step 4

Once the tomato past has cooked down add the vodka to the pan and let simmer.

step 5

Once the Vodka has cooked off add heavy cream, cheese, and more red pepper flakes for taste.

step 6

Add the cooked pasta and 1/4 cup of pasta water and mix until noodles are fully coated.

ingredients

Ingredients Ammounts
Rigatoni Pasta: 1 box
Tomato paste: 1-2 tubes
Vodka: 1/4 cup
Heavy Cream: 1/3 cup
Onion: 1/2 onion diced
Garlic: 3 cloves
Red pepper flakes: 1 tsp
Salt: to taste
Pepper: to taste
Butter: 1/2 stick

tools

Large Sauce Pan

Cooking Utensil

Cutting Board

Knife for Cutting

Measuring Cups
